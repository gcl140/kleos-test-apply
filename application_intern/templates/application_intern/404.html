About Us

TanSAF is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping Tanzanian students secure admission and financial aid at universities abroad. Its resource center offers free access to university materials, guidance, and support, including assistance with registration for standardized tests like the ACT, SAT, TOEFL, and IELTS.
